About Us

Industrial Maintenance Experts

Falcon Plant Resources is a multiservice company that provides a variety of mechanical, specialty welding, torque, and bolt tensioning services.

Our technicians are trained to handle projects for areas and industries such as: wind energy, oil refineries, heavy machinery, pulp, maritime ports, and engineering & construction.

Falcon Plant Resources brings over 25 years of proven expertise to the oil & gas, petrochemical, and refinery industries.
