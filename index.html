Management Commitment

Safety starts at the top.

Our leadership doesn't delegate safety—it drives it. From executive-level accountability to hands-on field visibility, we embed safety into every level of the organization. We invest in the latest safety technology, enforce strict jobsite standards, and lead with a zero-tolerance approach to shortcuts or compromises.

  • Direct involvement from leadership in audits and jobsite reviews
  • Safety KPIs (Safety Key Performance Indicators) tied to performance and project milestones

Safety Program

Customized protection for every project.

No two sites are alike—and neither are their risks. That's why our safety program is engineered to adapt. We conduct detailed hazard assessments, align with client protocols, and implement strict permit-to-work procedures, ensuring every phase is planned, monitored, and controlled.

  • Project-specific safety plans and task hazard analysis
  • Daily job safety briefings and stop-work authority for all personnel
  • Continuous auditing to ensure regulatory and client compliance

Employee Safety Training

Skilled hands. Safety-trained minds.

We believe well-trained workers are our strongest safety asset. Every crew member is certified and equipped to work confidently in hazardous environments. From onboarding to advanced scenario drills, our training program builds awareness, leadership, and rapid response capability.

  • Refinery-specific onboarding and role-based certifications

  • Behavioral-based safety reinforcement through coaching and observation

Controlled Services

Precision bolting and machining solutions for your most critical industrial applications.

Torque Services

Precision torque application services to ensure proper fastener tension and joint integrity in critical applications.

  • Accurate tensioning
  • Critical joint integrity
  • Leak-free performance

Bolt Tensioning

Advanced bolt tensioning services for flanged joints, ensuring uniform load distribution and preventing leaks.

  • Uniform load distribution
  • Leak prevention
  • Flange joint integrity

Nut Splitting

Stuck nuts and seized fasteners can bring critical operations to a halt. Our nut splitting services offer a fast, safe, and damage-free solution for removing corroded or frozen nuts without compromising the integrity of studs, bolts, or flanges.

  • Hydraulic precision tools
  • Zero damage to threads
  • Hot work elimination

Ultrasonic Bolt Monitoring

Ensure critical joint integrity with precision and confidence. Our Ultrasonic Bolt Monitoring service uses advanced sound wave technology to accurately measure bolt elongation, preload, and tension in real-time—without disassembly.

  • Real-time tension data
  • Non-destructive testing
  • Certified technicians

Drilling & Tapping

Precision drilling and tapping services for critical applications. Whether you're preparing parts for assembly, fabrication, or custom machining, our services are tailored to meet your exact specifications.

  • High-tolerance work
  • All materials
  • Emergency services

Flange Facing

Ensure flawless sealing and extended equipment life with our expert flange facing services. We specialize in restoring damaged or worn flange surfaces to original manufacturer specifications—or better.

  • On-site machining
  • Corrosion repair
  • Precision alignment

Beveling Services

At Falcon Plant Resources, we specialize in precision beveling for a wide range of materials including steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and alloys. Whether you're preparing pipes for high-strength welds or reducing sharp edges for improved handling, our advanced beveling techniques ensure consistency, accuracy, and compliance with industry standards.

  • Material-specific beveling
  • Weld preparation
  • Edge finishing

Our Fabrication Capabilities

ASME S Stamp Certification

Authorizes Falcon to fabricate and assemble power boilers and perform certain code-compliant repairs or alterations under Section I of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code (BPVC).

With our S Stamp certification, we can fabricate:
  • Power boilers and related components
  • Superheaters and economizers
  • Boiler external piping and fittings
  • Pressure parts for power boilers
  • Code-compliant repairs to Section I equipment
