Our Quality Standard

At Falcon Plant Resources, quality is at the core of every service we deliver—from precision welding and fabrication to mechanical installations and controlled bolting.

We are fully certified with:

  • ASME U & S Stamps – Pressure Vessel & Boiler Fabrication
  • NBIC R Stamp – Pressure Equipment Repairs
  • NB Stamp – Quality Control Program Registration

Highlights

  • Certified welders, ASME/API code-compliant
  • Full QA/QC with MTRs, weld maps & NDT
  • Calibrated torque equipment with post-job logs
  • Fabrication with complete traceability and dimensional checks

QA/QC Deliverables

Every job is supported with a complete Quality Turnover Package:

  • Inspection & Test Plans (ITPs)
  • Welder Certifications & WPS/PQRs
  • Torque Charts & Bolt Logs
  • NDT Reports (RT, UT, PT, MT)
  • Material Test Reports (MTRs)
  • Weld Maps & Traceability Logs
