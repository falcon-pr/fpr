Precision Torque Solutions

Our torque services are performed with hydraulic and/or electric machines for a high variety of bolt diameters and a wide range of torque values. We use different tool formats according to technical needs and work environments.

At Falcon Plant Resources, we understand that proper torque application is critical for equipment integrity, safety, and performance. Our certified technicians use state-of-the-art equipment to ensure precise torque application every time.
