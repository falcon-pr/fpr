Certified Fabrication & Repair Services

At Falcon Plant Resources, we deliver fully certified fabrication and repair services for pressure vessels, process piping, and structural components — ensuring strict compliance with ASME and NBIC codes.

As an ASME U Stamp and NBIC R Stamp certified facility, we are authorized to fabricate new pressure-retaining equipment and perform code-compliant repairs and alterations, both in-shop and in the field.

With experienced fabricators, certified welders, advanced QA/QC systems, and full traceability, we deliver turnkey fabrication and repair solutions trusted by refineries, petrochemical plants, terminals, and industrial facilities across the nation.

Whether building new systems or restoring existing ones, we ensure safety, code compliance, and operational reliability — every time.

Our Fabrication Services Include:

Pressure Vessel Fabrication

ASME Section VIII compliant fabrication of new pressure vessels with full U Stamp certification

Modular Skids & Packages

Custom fabrication of modular process skids, equipment frames, and integrated packages

Pipe Fabrication

Precision pipe spool fabrication for carbon steel, stainless, and high-alloy materials

Custom Tanks & Drums

Fabrication of storage tanks, air receivers, knock-out drums, and specialty vessels

Weld Repairs & Modifications

Component replacement, nozzle modifications, and structural alterations

Heat Exchanger Services

Re-tubing, channel/shell repairs, and component replacement for heat exchangers
