Bolt Tensioning Services

Precision hydraulic tensioning solutions for reliable, consistent bolted connections in industrial applications

Precision Bolt Tensioning

At FPR we have a wide range of suitable and quality tools to carry out all types of tensioning work including hydraulic tensioners with different metrics, hydraulic pumps, hoses and accessories.

Bolt tensioning is an advanced technique that offers a more precise and controlled way to apply torque to bolts. This provides more reliable and consistent connections compared to hand tightening or hydraulic torque wrenches. Our highly qualified personnel ensure safe and effective bolt tensioning for all your industrial needs.

Why Choose Bolt Tensioning?

Superior Precision

Turnbuckles offer more precise tensioning than traditional methods, ensuring accurate bolt loading every time.

Faster Operation

Our tensioning systems work significantly faster than manual methods, reducing downtime.

Stable Performance

Hydraulic tensioners provide stable, consistent performance even in demanding environments.

Easy Control

Our systems offer precise control over tensioning parameters for optimal joint integrity.

Bolt tensioning is typically the perfect choice when sealing a critical bolted joint that requires precise, reliable tension for optimal performance and safety.
