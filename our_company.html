About Falcon Plant Resources

At Falcon Plant Resources (FPR), we are dedicated to providing mechanical, specialty welding, torque, and tensioning services using specialized tools and equipment in a variety of industries.

With over 25 years of experience in the United States market, our objective is to offer quality solutions and service as safely as possible.

Our field personnel are well-trained to avoid accidents and optimize project timelines. Our extensive portfolio of equipment includes hydraulic, electric, cordless, and manual torque tools, hydraulic tensioners, and torque & tensioning pumps, among others.

Contact Falcon Plant Resources today to learn how we can assist with your project needs.

Our Commitment to Safety & Quality

Our core focus is ensuring the highest standards of safety and quality throughout the industry. Our commitment begins with training our employees to complete projects safely and efficiently, resulting in a proven track record of on-time and incident-free project completions. While we prioritize professionalism, our main goal is to achieve accident-free projects that exceed quality benchmarks.

We adhere to safety procedures and policies in line with modern standards, striving to go above and beyond to prevent unforeseen incidents. Internally, our technicians undergo ongoing safety training, emphasizing the importance of safe practices.

Regardless of project size, safety and quality are our top priorities. Choosing Falcon Plant Resources means you are choosing to work with some of the most highly trained and safety-conscious specialists in the industry.
